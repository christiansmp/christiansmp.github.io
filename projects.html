Development

Current Focus

We are in active development, preparing the infrastructure and systems that will support our community. Our focus is on building a stable, welcoming, and spiritually healthy environment.

Technical Priorities

  • Server infrastructure and performance optimization
  • Whitelist and application system development
  • Discord integration and community management tools
  • Custom plugins for community features
  • Backup and data protection systems
  • Anti-grief and moderation tooling
  • Website and documentation
Roadmap

Upcoming Features

Experiments and features we are working toward.

01

Spawn Cathedral

A community-built cathedral at spawn that will serve as the heart of the server, featuring stained glass, an altar, and space for gatherings.

02

Scripture Signs

A plugin allowing players to place signs with Scripture verses that rotate daily, encouraging meditation on God's Word as players explore.

03

Prayer Request Board

An in-game system for submitting and viewing prayer requests, helping the community intercede for one another.

04

Community Events

Regular server-wide events including building competitions, scavenger hunts, and collaborative projects with spiritual themes.

05

Voice Chapel

Integration with Discord to enable in-game voice proximity chat in designated chapel areas for impromptu fellowship.

06

Seasonal Celebrations

Special decorations and activities for Advent, Lent, Easter, and other significant seasons in the Christian calendar.

Vision

Long-Term Direction

Our vision extends beyond a typical gaming server. We are building a digital parish, a place where believers can gather across time zones and continents to worship, learn, and grow together.

Ultimately, we pray that Christian SMP becomes a place where Christ is exalted, the saints are edified, and the watching world sees something beautiful: believers dwelling together in unity.
