Ministry Vision

Our purpose is discipleship in community

Christian SMP exists so that following Jesus is supported, not lonely. We cultivate a world where believers can grow in faith, strengthen one another, and build with joy.

Art Placeholder

Room for classical imagery reflecting the mission.

Our Heart as Staff

Servants, not titles

Every staff member is a volunteer who plays survival alongside the community. We do not power-trip, we do not chase titles, and we serve because the Father sees what is done in secret.

Jesus is the true head of this community. Our goal is to shepherd gently and keep the focus on Christ.

Community Direction

Transparent decisions

Major decisions are communicated clearly. Staff branches discuss and vote with YES / NO / AMEND, and when needed we invite the wider community into prayerful input.

Staff Structure

Five volunteer branches

Content

Crafts resources, devotionals, and prompts that keep Scripture central.

Fellowship

Hosts community nights, small groups, and relational touchpoints.

Builder

Designs the spawn, landmarks, and shared spaces that inspire worshipful creativity.

Moderation

Protects the community with grace, fairness, and clear accountability.

Developer

Maintains plugins, tools, and the technical health of the server.

Roles

Shepherds, volunteers, witnesses

Shepherds care for spiritual health, Volunteers serve with humility, and Witnesses help others meet Jesus through authentic community.

Encouragement

Serve with rest

We serve Christ, not a position. Take sabbaticals when needed, lean on grace, and remember that faithfulness is more valuable than visibility.
